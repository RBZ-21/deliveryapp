Our Story

Built from both sides of the dock

For 20 years, I worked in some of the finest restaurants in the greater Charleston, SC area. Every morning started the same way: wondering when the produce truck would show up, whether the fish delivery would land before lunch service, and whether today would be the day a driver showed up to the wrong entrance again.

Then I made the jump to the other side — joining a seafood wholesale distributor that supplied the same upscale restaurants I'd spent two decades working in. Suddenly I was the one building routes, dispatching drivers, and fielding calls from receiving managers who just wanted a straight answer on ETAs.

The frustration was identical from both positions. Restaurants couldn't plan around deliveries they couldn't predict. Distributors couldn't communicate live location without a dozen phone calls. The tools to fix this existed — nobody had connected them.

DeliverEase is what I wish I'd had in both jobs: a simple, real-time link between the driver on the road and the person waiting at the dock. No app installs for customers. No complex setup. Just clarity.
